Free Writer

This is a web text editor made in JavaScript that allows you to instantly scramble all the words on your page, so you can keep your work and notes away from any nosy, prying eyes. Press the 'secure' button on the homepage to hide your true writing, then click the 'visible' button to change it back to normal. To quickly turn scrambling on or off while you're typing, press the "CTRL" key.

You'll want to click on / use the 'secure' feature multiple times until the length of the scrambled texts is to your liking.

Free Writer is designed to be minimally distracting, with a clean, elegant aesthetic so that the app can blend in with whatever working environment you may find yourself in. You will very soon be able to save your text notes and export them as PDF files.

Try Free Writer now on any device capable of browsing the web, and discover the peace of mind of being able to put down your most sensitive, guarded thoughts in public when you can safely hide all your secrets with a quick touch of a keyboard.
